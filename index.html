What path awaits you?

The game tells us the story of Kai – the Last Guardian of light, who alone stopped the forces of Darkness.
This is a story about finding yourself, your place in life, overcoming obstacles to achieve your goals.
This is a story about how a small fragile light being is forced to fight against the vast all-consuming darkness and only by a miracle to win.
This is a story about hope, hope that no matter what, one day everything will be all right.
